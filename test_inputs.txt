## 📋 Sample Symptom Descriptions for Testing

You can copy and paste these into the SymptomAssist chat interface to test the reasoning engine:

### 1. 🌡️ Influenza (Flu) Test Case
"I have developed a sudden high fever of 102°F. My entire body aches, and I feel extremely exhausted/fatigued. I also have a dry cough and chills."

### 2. 🤕 Migraine Test Case
"I have a severe headache that throbs on only the left side of my head. I feel nauseous and am very sensitive to light and loud sounds right now."

### 3. 🍖 Food Poisoning / Stomach Bug Test Case
"I'm experiencing intense stomach cramps and have vomited three times since this morning. Several others who ate the same suspect meal with me last night are also feeling ill."

### 4. 🚽 UTI Test Case
"It really burns whenever I go to the bathroom (urinate). I also feel like I have to go very frequently, but only a small amount comes out."

### 5. 🏠 Common Cold Test Case
"I've had a runny nose and have been sneezing all day. My throat feels a bit scratchy, but I don't have a fever or any major body aches."

### 6. ❗ Emergency / Red Flag Test Case (Chest Pain)
"I'm having a tight, crushing feeling in my chest that's radiating to my left arm. I'm also sweating a lot and finding it a bit hard to breathe."
